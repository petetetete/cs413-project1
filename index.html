Just a Regular Tuesday

Instructions

Welcome to just a regular tuesday. There is absolutely nothing abnormal with anything that is happening around you. What? You see a ghost looking thing in some sort of cellar-like dungeon? I have no idea what you're talking about, this is just a regular Tuesday full of regular Tuesday things.

Use either the arrow keys or wasd to control our hero, avoiding the perils around him for as long as possible. From the game's Rogue roots, the enemies only move when you move, so it's up to you whether this is a game of strategy, or running-and-gunning.
