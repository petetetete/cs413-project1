Just a Regular Tuesday

Instructions

W or ↑ - Move up one space A or ← - Move left one space
S or ↓ - Move down one space D or → - Move right one space

Today is just a regular Tuesday. There is absolutely nothing abnormal with anything that is happening around you. What? You see a ghost looking thing in some sort of cellar-like dungeon? I have no idea what you're talking about, this is just a regular Tuesday full of regular Tuesday things.

Use either the arrow keys or wasd to control the everyday hero, avoiding the perils around him for as long as possible. This rogue-like, single-screen, survival game will test just how well you can plan ahead and dodge hordes of pixelated foes. Similar to the genre's namesake, Rogue, the enemies only move when you move, so it's up to you whether you plan out methodically, or sprint around like a mad-man.
